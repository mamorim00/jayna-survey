Summer Internship Application — Eligibility Screening

Please complete all qualification questions accurately. False declarations will result in immediate disqualification and possible referral to the Academic Integrity Office.

QUESTION 01 / 10
Are you a current student at Aalto University?
This internship is available exclusively to enrolled Aalto students.
QUESTION 02 / 10
Is your current GPA above 3.5 on the Finnish 5-point scale?
Academic performance is a key eligibility criterion.
QUESTION 03 / 10
Have you completed all mandatory safety trainings, including the mandatory 4-hour online module on Ergonomic Keyboard Posture (EKP-101)?
All interns must be EKP-101 certified before commencing employment.
QUESTION 04 / 10
Do you own at least one (1) business-casual cardigan?
Aalto's summer internship dress code requires a minimum cardigan count of one. Hoodies do not qualify unless they have buttons.
QUESTION 05 / 10
Can you confirm you have not, in the past 12 months, eaten a kolmioleipä while standing up?
Our workplace wellness policy prohibits this for ergonomic and crumb-related liability reasons.
QUESTION 06 / 10
Have you memorised the entire Aalto University sustainability pledge in both Finnish and Swedish, and can you recite it backwards?
This is standard onboarding. Most candidates complete the memorisation in 6–8 months.
QUESTION 07 / 10
Do you currently have a valid Emotional Support Plant (ESP) registered with the university's Office of Intern Wellbeing?
ESP registration ensures a nurturing intern environment. Cacti qualify only if named.
QUESTION 08 / 10
Are you able to attend all 14 mandatory pre-internship orientation sessions, including the 6 AM Nordic Walking Kickoff on June 2nd?
Attendance is tracked via GPS ankle monitor. Loaner monitors are not available.
QUESTION 09 / 10
Have you ever, intentionally or unintentionally, made eye contact with a seagull on Otaniemi campus?
Due to an ongoing situation with the campus seagulls, applicants who have engaged in direct ocular contact may pose a liability risk.
QUESTION 10 / 10
Can you certify that your LinkedIn profile photo was taken within the past 30 days, features exactly 1.5 smiles (neither full nor straight-faced), and is sized 400×400 pixels exactly?
Our HR system automatically rejects non-compliant profile photos. 1.4 or 1.6 smiles are not acceptable.
